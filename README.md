### About Me

- Currently building AI-powered SaaS applications - image generation tools, token checkers, and multiverse-style AI apps.
- Part of the MirAI School of Technology - Virtual Summer Internship 2026, AI Builder Track.
- In my free time I dig into CTF challenges - forensics, steganography, OSINT, and web exploitation.
- Comfortable across the stack: Python/Streamlit for quick AI apps, Next.js/React/Firebase for full products, and Linux/bash for forensic and security tooling.
- Always learning - most recently going deep on disk forensics, LSB steganography, and prompt-injection defenses.
